Trekzi

Trekzi was first founded in Jan 2019 during a startup hackathon.

A team of random high school students created the idea over 48 hours, and I decided to implement it with CS50W!
BTW Our team was fortunate enough to come first place!
We received $500 as a cash prize and are going further with our idea.
Please reach out if you'd like to offer advises/help!

We are travellers who knows how hard it is to plan a trip. A thousand things can go wrong!
With Trekzi, we are hoping to deliver a personalised travel itinerary in an instant.
Our Minimum Viable Product is soon to come!
